Service Website Cost Breakdown

This breakdown explains the essential components required to build, secure, and properly maintain a professional service website.

Item Cost (₦) Why This Is Necessary
Website Hosting ₦80,000 Hosting keeps your website online 24/7. It ensures fast loading speed, good performance, and reliability for visitors.
Domain Name ₦35,000 The domain is your website’s unique address on the internet and gives your business a professional and trusted identity.
SSL Certificate ₦20,000 SSL protects user data, enables HTTPS, builds customer trust, and is required for search engine ranking.
Premium Plugins & Tools ₦40,000 These tools provide security, backups, performance optimization, contact forms, and advanced website functionality.
Setup, Configuration & Maintenance Buffer ₦25,000 Covers professional setup, configuration, and testing, to ensure smooth operation.
Total Cost: ₦200,000
Important: The total cost reflects not just tools, but professional setup, security, stability, and long-term reliability required for a service website.
